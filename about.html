About Us

About Us

We borne here………..as Environmental Research Forum

We feel the nature within us and around us as a heavenly gift for the planet for all creatures. The bountiful of flora and fauna along with many invisible microbial communities supported by the most precious oxygen, water and many other innumerable resources makes the planet beautiful and wonderful beyond imagination.

The life supporting system of the planet is organized at its ultimate level and visible in the beauty and very presence of all the beings along with the inanimate objects all around. The flowing perennial streams and rivers, the fathomless ocean, sky touching mountains, bright sunrays, cool moonlight, soothing breeze, brilliant colours and forms of beautiful life makes the planet worth living with the value uncountable.

But, multiplicative anthropogenic activities in the name of development bringing large scale damage to the environment today spoil the beauty of nature. Voluminous wastes and pollutants accumulated in our surrounding ruining the essence of air, water and soil. We are the only creatures pulling the planet towards ultimate state of disorderness and causing the extinction of many species and pushing many others species to the stage of the verge of their extinction.

We are a group of individuals in the name of TEAM ERF with immense love for nature setting up our marvelous plan to build a GLOBAL ENVIRONMENT FAMILY to aware the people at all corners about the value of our beautiful environment, sharing and transfer of knowledge to form the community to think and rethink and take intelligent action to protect our environment and make the life sustainable in the planet.

We borne local but working global for the sake of our Dharti mata (Mother Earth) for whom we are here……….
