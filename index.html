Founder’s Voice

Our nature is our mother from whom we borne and grown up in her lap, nourished and took a magical form of life which flourished thereafter. For our valued survival since our origin we got oxygen to breathe, food to eat, home to stay. Not only that we got every facilities for healthy and sustainable living. But, what we are giving to our mother in return? Only pollution and huge quantities of waste. In the wake of developmental activities of human civilization our environment is irreversibly damaged and life of other species is in the stake. Many species became extinct and many more are in endangered or critically endangered position and in the verge of extinction. Planet may be beautiful without us but for our evil deed we may not live long in this planet. Before causing huge scale damage beyond resilience, let us pledge to shoulder responsibility to make use of the brilliant gift of nature for our own sake and the entire life on the planet for healthy and sustainable co-existence.

Founder and Director
Environmenenal Research Forum (ERF)

AIM

i. Creating awareness regarding the architecture of our beautiful environment
ii. Educate people at all level to live in harmony with the environment for their own survival following the principle “save planet save yourself.”

OBJECTIVE

i. Determined to build and maintain sustainable ecosystem by reducing anthropogenic interventions
ii. Promoting environmental education as a part of basic education in schools, colleges, universities and all social establishments through mass participation

GOAL

i. Development of eco-friendly technologies to reverse the impact of global climate change
ii. To work for environemtal research, publications, dissemination of information, knowledge sharing through collaboration at all levels of the society around the world
iii. Welcoming the people around the world to advocate for their environment to make our earth beautiful since the inception and take responsibility to protect the womb which nourishes all creatures in the planet
